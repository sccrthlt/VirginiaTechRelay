Greeks Candles System

VT Relay Candles for Greeks is a chapter based competition that rewards points (candles) to companies that engage in Relay activities. Participants can win points for their company in four ways: receive donations, sign people up for Relay in your chapter, attend Relay events, and buy Greek Relay t-shirts.

Donations

Have your company raise the 1st, 2nd, or 3rd most money on April, 19th and your company will win a special prize. There are no candles awarded for donations, only prizes on April 19th.

Registration

Have a certain percentage of your company signed up for Relay by a specific date to receive candles. The percentages and dates are specified here.

Events

Attend VT Relay For Life events to receive candles. Write your name down at the cash register to receive candles for attending. Events include: Percentage nights, kickoff, team meetings, etc. Upcoming events will be posted on the home page of this website.

Hokie Passport support coming soon!

T-shirts

Have a certain percentage of your company buy designated Greek Relay t-shirts to receive candles. Greek Relay shirts are listed here.

Ex. 20% of your company buys the bar crawl t-shirt. Your company receives 300 candles.
