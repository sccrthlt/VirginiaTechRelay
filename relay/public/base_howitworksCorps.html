Corps Candles System

VT Relay Candles is a company-based competition that rewards points (candles) to companies that engage in Relay activities. Participants can win points for their company in three ways: achieve milestones by receiving donations, send emails, and attend Relay events.

Milestones

A milestone is a level of money raised. There are several milestones. Each milestone you pass you will receive the milestone dollar amount in candles.

Ex. You have reached the $100 milestone because you have raised $110. You will receive 100 candles for reaching the $100 milestone.

Emails

Send emails through the vtrelay.org website to receive candles. Each email you send you will receive 10 candles. Send as many as you want.

Ex. You have sent 10 emails through the vtrelay.org website. You will receive 100 candles.

Events

Attend VT Relay For Life events to receive candles. Write your name down to at the cash register to receive candles for attending. Events include: Percentage nights, kickoff, team meetings, etc.

Description

A milestone is a level of money raised. There are several milestones. Each milestone you pass you will receive the milestone dollar amount in candles.

Ex. You have reached the $100 milestone because you have raised $110. You will receive 100 candles for reaching the $100 milestone. You will NOT receive 10 extra candles for raising $10 over the milestone. The next time you receive candles will be when you have raised a total of $250. You will receive an additional 250 candles for surpassing this milestone. You will then have a grand total of 350 candles.

Emails Candle Value

1 email = 30 candles

Description

The number one fundraising tool is emails sent by participants. On average, one email raises $28 towards the cure.

Each email you send through the vtrelay.org website, your company will receive 30 candles. Send as many as you want.

Instructions to send emails on vtrelay.org:

1. Login

2. Scroll down until you see My Tools

3. Click on compose an email
